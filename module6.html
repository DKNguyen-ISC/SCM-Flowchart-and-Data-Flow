Logic & Data Definitions

Snapshot Logic

Stock_Reconciliation does not compare against "Live" stock. It compares against a "System Snapshot" taken at the exact time the count started to avoid timing errors.

Decoupled Audit

M6 is separate from M5. Operations (M5) keep moving. Audits (M6) run in parallel and inject corrections (Adjustments) into the stream when finalized.

Primary Keys

Reconciliation is unique by BOM_UPDATE + LOCATION. Adjustments are tracked by a unique ADJUSTMENT_ID for financial auditability.
