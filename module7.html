Logic & Data Definitions

The 3-Way Match

The system validates:
1. Price (from PO - M2)
2. Qty (from Receipt - M5)
3. Total (from Invoice - M7)

Cash Protection

Because Material_Receipt (M5) only logs "Good Stock," Finance never pays for rejected or short-shipped items automatically. The variance forces a manual review.

Data Keys

Payment_Request links back to PO_LINE_ID to ensure line-item accuracy. Partial payments are tracked via REQUEST_ID.
