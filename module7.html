MODULE 7: FINANCE & PAYABLES

Process Overview (v14.0)

This is the final module where the process is financially closed.
Key Logic: The system performs a **3-Way Match** by comparing the `Purchase_Order` (M2), the `Material_Receipt` (M5), and the `Supplier_Invoice` (M7). This match is required to approve a `Payment_Request` and issue a `Payment`.

1. Supplier_Invoice [H]

The process is triggered when an external `Supplier_Invoice` is received from the supplier. This is the "bill" we must pay.
Note: The supplier for `PO-100` has incorrectly billed us for the full **26,000** units on Line 3, even though we were short-shipped and rejected 1,500 units in total.

2. Payment_Request (3-Way Match) [H]

This is the **core financial control**. The system generates a `Payment_Request` and compares three data sources for each invoice line:
1. **PO Qty (M2):** What we *ordered*.
2. **Accepted Qty (M5):** What we *received and passed QC* (from `Material_Receipt`).
3. **Invoice Qty (M7):** What the supplier is *billing us* for.

3. Payment

Only the lines that were "Approved" in the 3-Way Match are sent to the payment system (e.g., bank transfer). The "Blocked" line (`PO-100, L3`) is *not* paid, preventing an overpayment of 1,500 units.
