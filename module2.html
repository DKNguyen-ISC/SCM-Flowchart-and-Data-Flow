Logic & Data Definitions

Status Logic

Purchase_Order status is updated by downstream events. It starts as 'Sent', moves to 'In-Transit' (via ASN in M4), and finally 'Closed' (via Receipt in M5).

Consolidation Pattern

This is a N:1 relationship. Multiple Purchase_Requests are merged into one Purchase_Order to reduce shipping costs.

Sourcing Strategy

Sourcing is decoupled from Planning (M1). Planning defines *what* (Tech Spec). Procurement defines *who* (Commercial Term).
