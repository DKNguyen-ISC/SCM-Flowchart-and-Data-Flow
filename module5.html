Module 5

Inventory Operations

The Execution Engine. This module handles the physical movement of goods based on M4's QC Decision. It updates the Stock Ledger (History) and Stock Data (Current State).

Logic & Data Definitions

Double-Entry Inventory

Just like accounting, every move has a source and a destination. The Stock_Ledger tracks the net change (+/-) to keep balances accurate.

Quarantine Logic

Quarantined stock is "On Hand" (physically in the building) but "Unavailable" (cannot be used for M3 Planning). This prevents bad stock from covering demand.

The Execution Loop

M5 is the execution arm. M1/M2/M3 plan the work; M4/M5 do the work. The result (Stock_Data) feeds back into M3 for the next planning cycle.
